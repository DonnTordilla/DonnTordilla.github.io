Going vegan!

Posted on 3/24/2023

The other day, Imee and I watched a documentary on Netflix called "What the Health". Imee was hoping to find healthier alternatives for all the things she was concerned about in my diet. Part of her now regrets playing that documentary.

Instead of just suggesting alternatives to a select number of food items, the documentary is essentially an all-out attack on the consumption of animal products. To my surprise, I bought into the message. Now, just as the title says, we're going vegan!

I'm a lot more excited about this change than Imee is. She's much more of a carnivore than I am, or more accurately, she's kind of a picky eater, and I'm not. Still, like the documentary says, "Nothing tastes as good as healthy feels," and Imee is willing to try this diet for the sake of being healthy. I hope this change works for us!
